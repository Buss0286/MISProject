Target Companies: JPMorgan Chase & Goldman Sachs

What is Equity Research?

Equity research analysts conduct in-depth financial analysis of companies and industries to provide investment recommendations to institutional clients. This role involves:

  • Financial modeling and valuation analysis (DCF, comparables, precedent transactions)
  • Industry research and competitive analysis
  • Company site visits and management meetings
  • Earnings analysis and quarterly report reviews
  • Writing research reports and price targets
  • Interacting with institutional investors and sales teams
